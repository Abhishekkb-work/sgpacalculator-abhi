About Us

Welcome to the SGPA Calculator website created by Abhishek Bagewadi. This platform is designed to help students quickly and accurately calculate their Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) based on their academic performance.

Whether you're a college student trying to keep track of your grades or simply exploring your academic progress, this tool provides a simple and efficient solution. It eliminates the need for manual calculations and reduces the chances of errors.

I believe that technology can make student life easier — and this SGPA calculator is a small step in that direction. Thank you for using the tool, and feel free to share it with your friends and classmates!

Purpose

Our vision is to simplify academic life by providing tools that help students save time and stay informed about their performance. Through this SGPA calculator, we aim to make academic calculations accessible, accurate, and stress-free for every student.
